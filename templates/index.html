How does the AI personalize my learning plan?

Amadeus adapts to your goals, level, and pace using your inputs and practice results. Your plan updates automatically with bite‑size lessons, quizzes, and reviews that fit your day.

Is my data private?

Yes. We’re private by design. Your learning data is protected and never sold. You decide what’s stored and can export or delete anytime.

What is Camera Mode?

Camera Mode lets you rehearse presentations, practice pronunciation, or do whiteboard tasks—anywhere. Get time‑boxed prompts and feedback to improve faster.

How do streaks and calendar help?

The calendar tracks your study streak and milestones to keep you motivated. See what to review each day and celebrate progress.

Can I ask questions in natural language?

Absolutely. Chat with the AI like a tutor—ask anything and get clear explanations, examples, and mini‑quizzes tailored to you.

Do you support different subjects?

Yes. Popular areas include languages, math, science, presentations, and more. Your plan is customized to your topics.
